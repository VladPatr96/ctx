# ctx — Complex Task eXecution

## Overview
A multi-provider context management and AI orchestration platform that empowers developers (CLI Power Users, Enterprise AI Teams, and Solopreneurs) to efficiently manage and optimize their AI-driven workflows across multiple LLM providers.

## Vision
To provide a seamless, unified interface for AI interactions, ensuring that context is preserved, tasks are routed to the most capable models, and project-specific knowledge is continuously accumulated and synced.

## Core Features
- **Adaptive Routing:** Intelligently routes tasks to the best-performing AI provider based on historical data, cost, and latency.
- **Consilium (CBDP):** Implements the Claim-Based Deliberation Protocol for multi-round, anonymous consensus building among different AI models.
- **Syncable Knowledge Base:** Maintains a cross-project knowledge base using SQLite FTS5, synchronized through GitHub Issues for effortless collaboration and versioning.
- **Desktop Dashboard:** A robust desktop application built with Electron, React, and Vite, providing a visual control center for all AI-powered task executions.

## Target Audience
- **CLI Power Users:** Developers who prefer terminal-based workflows but want the power of multiple AI models.
- **Enterprise AI Teams:** Organizations looking to standardize AI usage, monitor performance, and optimize costs.
- **Solopreneurs:** Individual creators who need a unified, intelligent assistant that learns from every project.

## Success Metrics
- Reduction in task execution latency by optimizing provider selection.
- Increase in the quality and consistency of AI-generated responses through the Consilium protocol.
- Effortless reuse of project-specific lessons via the integrated knowledge base.
